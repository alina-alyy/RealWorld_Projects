Know About Covid-19

To All Our Lovely Customer

Here at the Milton Spice we go to extreme lengths to ensure our food is prepared and cooked hygienically.

We take great pride in our 5* Food Hygiene rating.

However, with the COVID-19, we wish to emphasise to all our existing and new customers that hygiene has always been taken seriously here at The Milton Spice.

Our vastly experienced kitchen staff, delivery drivers and management have all been briefed and informed of ways to stay safe and clean whilst providing you all with our great service during these testing times.

Milton Spice are open as usual with the option of Home delivery or Collection until further notice.
